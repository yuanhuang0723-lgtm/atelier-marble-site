# Google Ads Keyword Structure

Objective: generate high-intent B2B inquiries for luxury marble and stone export projects.

## Campaign Start Rule

Start with the high-intent buyer keywords only. Use a small test budget and optimize for inquiry quality, WhatsApp conversations, drawings received, and quote readiness rather than click volume.

## Ad Groups

### Custom Countertops

Landing page: `/kitchen-countertops`

Must-bid keywords:

- custom marble countertop manufacturer
- marble kitchen countertop China supplier
- custom stone fabrication company
- luxury villa stone countertops

Ad copy direction:

- Headline: Custom Marble Countertop Manufacturer
- Description: Premium stone fabrication for luxury kitchens and villas. Share drawings and request project pricing from China.

## Negative Keywords

Exclude completely:

- free marble
- DIY stone
- cheap countertop
- stone tiles low price
- low price
- second hand
- repair
- job
- salary
- course

## Conversion Rule

Every ad group should send traffic to its matching landing page, not only the homepage. Each landing page includes:

- one clear hero CTA
- WhatsApp click path
- short inquiry form
- real enhanced source-image proof
- internal links to project and material references
